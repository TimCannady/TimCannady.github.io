Welcome!

I'm a web enthusiast and a student at Dev Bootcamp. Developing websites is such a rewarding hobby. For me it crosses a three-way intersection of my passions for building things, for computers and for ecommerce.

On the surface I use this site to discuss web technologies, work culture and personal development. Yet it's so much more! It's also:

  • a practice in designing and hand-coding a website
  • a practice in managing a website
  • a practice in teaching
  • a practice in giving back

Thanks for visiting. Enjoy, and feel free to get in touch.
